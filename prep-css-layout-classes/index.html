Spider-Men

Spider-Man

Tobey Maguire stars in this 2002 film. Being the first movie actor to portray the web-slinger and gains the nostalgic factor.

The Amazing Spider-Man

10 years later, Andrew Garfield picks up the role and perfectly gets the 'quipping' of Spider-Man. This movie series also gets the physics of the web-swinging right! (Andrew Garfield is my favorite Spider-Man)

Spider-Man:Homecoming

Tom Holland is the newest spider in town, portraying the youngest Peter Parker and also being the closest in age to Peter. He portrays the most accurate Peter Parker so far!
